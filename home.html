How To Play "SJ SPOT & WIN"

“SJ SPOT & WIN” is based on the most popular sport in India - Cricket. It is an online game of skill inspired from Cricket.

The sport of cricket requires immense skill, not just to play but also to judge.

If you got the skill and the knowledge about cricket, “SJ SPOT & WIN” is the right place to test your skills.

It is a time based competition, All a user needs to do is observe the image carefully and Spot/Predict the position of the ball from the provided set of SPOTS on the playing screen.

A player can also take cues from the stats provided on the playing screen. As these stats and factors are taken into consideration by our ‘AI’ software systems to provide the Result/Outcome.

Pro Tip - Multiple Quantities and Spot selections are allowed to better the winning probabilities.
